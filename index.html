My Math IA is about modelling the cadaver cooling curve, or the relationship of the difference between cadaver and ambient temperature against time after death.

Read my IA about it!

However, when I wanted to model the relationship, it was hard to find information, or a specific resource that would help me easily depict it. Having spent a lot of time reading a lot of different articles and papers, which ate up my time, I thought I'd make this resource so my IA information is more accessible to people who want to learn more about this topic.

Dunno if anyone would ever be interested, but you can easily see a depiction of my data over here, as well as calculate the R^2 values of each model to see how effective they are! So it's like a mini-interactive IA haha.
